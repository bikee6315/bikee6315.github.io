About The Nascent Education

Nascent Education Foundation Pvt. Ltd has established in 2012 AD. and is a member of Educational consultancy Association of Nepal (ECAN) and NICE. Since 2013 it has been mainly focusing in Indian education. With TITI certified counsellors it has an enthusiastic and qualified team that recognizes the essence of quality education.

Within the short span of time, it has become synonymous to Indian education. The very thought of Indian education leads parents to our office. Currently we are the only organization solely dealing with the Indian education.

We believe in diligence and motivation for fulfillment of our goals. In most of the cases, we authenticate the institutions’ features and attributes through personal visits. As results, we provide the interested students first hand information about the schools/colleges through visual CD’s and pamphlets having details about institutions.

Our Objectives:

  • To guide our students to the best educational environment.
  • To provide our students a quality education.
  • To stand as a second guardian for entire study period.
  • To provide quality counseling which will help students to decide their future.
  • To build relationship with parents and students.

Currently our corporate office is located in Birgunj which is totally dedicated for Indian Education . Also we are planning to open branches in Kathmandu, Pokhara, Chitwan, Butwal, Lalbandi & Bhairahawa to provide services at their door step to the needy students and parents. Among these places Bhairahawa branch has successfully launched and performing its duty of counseling the students about Indian education.

Message From Director

Nascent Education Foundation or NEF, Birgunj was established with the motive of assisting students from South East Asian countries like Nepal, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Pakistan, Srilanka etc for further studies on their desired destination.

It has been working forward to place students on the courses of their choices providing them various alternatives abroad on the various courses like MBBS, BDS, BSc Nursing, Optometry, Engineering, Physiotheraphy, MPH and BPH and lot other courses on full funding basis.

NEF also focuses on providing the students with the information regarding the opportunities of scholarships, grants, trainings & more. NEF firmly believes on cordial poise between the students, colleges & stakeholders for the overall development of the educational sector (National/ International) ultimately for each to flourish and thence xecutes its purposeful and thence, executes its purposeful operation via multiple methodologies inclusive of Events, Campaigns, Networks (Socio/Commercial), Technology etc.

Our Mission

Being a bridge across cultures and support for academic pursuit of the younger generation is our mission.

Our experienced education counselors are ready to advise students on courses and career opportunities according to their educational background, interests, aptitude, and aspirations. Have a broad network with education providers; we are able to suggest a wide range of study options and programs to meet the students’ needs.

Nascent Education Foundation is an all round service provider. Our care and concern continue though the students have left their home country. Our Representatives in India/Bangladesh can offer first-hand assistance to students including airport pickup services, helping arrange home-stays and even opening bank account, local SIM Cards etc. We also stay in regular contact with our students and liaise with schools and parents in monitoring students’ progress.

Our Development

From a single office in Birgunj in 2013, Nascent Education Foundation has grown and expanded. As well as our Head Office in Birgunj, Now we have representative offices in Lalbandi, & Bhairahawa.

Nascent Education Foundation now represents in excess of 100+ education providers, including universities, colleges of India & Bangladesh.
