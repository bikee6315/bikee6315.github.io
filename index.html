Who We Are??

Nascent Education Foundation (also referred as NEF) is Top Educational Consultancy in Birgunj, Nepal established with the aim of assisting students of South-East Asia from countries like Nepal, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Pakistan, Srilanka,etc for higher studies in their dream destinations. We have been working with the students in shaping their careers by helping them get admission in professional courses. We offer professional courses like MBBS, BDS, BSc Nursing, Optometry, Engineering, Physiotheraphy, MPH and BPH and many more in many countries like India, Bangladesh, Australia and others.We have multiple programs like the Study in India program that makes the whole process of choosing a college much more convenient and hassle free. NEF keeps their students updated on various information and notifications regarding the opportunities of scholarships, Government grants, trainings, Exams & more. NEF firmly believes that a cordial relationship between the students and colleges will bring an overall development in Professional Education Sector. We constantly want to grow this domain through Events, Campaigns, Networks (Socio/Commercial), Technology etc.

We at Nascent Education Foundation believe that Education Consultancies have a very important role to play in the lives of students who want to pursue higher Education in professional Courses. Educational Consultancies have connections with both the students and the Colleges. Thus, Educationa Consultancies can work as a bridge to minimize the differences between these two. Both need each other, Colleges want the best students and Students want the best College for their professional course. We at NAscent Education as an Education Consultancy work towards making sure that both are satisfies with what they get. WE have establishes ourselves as the best Educational Consultancy in Birgunj and many other parts of Nepal. We are student's favourite when it comes to Education Consultancies, Parents Trust us and for students we are their second guardians. For Colleges we are the best Education Consultancy as not only we provide the best business but we help Colleges acquire the Top students from even the remotest parts. We are affliated with all the Top Colleges of India and Bangladesh. NEF has years of experience as Education Consultant and our hardwork and determination has resulted in us being the Best Consultancy.
