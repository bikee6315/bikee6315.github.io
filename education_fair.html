4th Indian Education Fair

Choosing a College for a professional course is a very big challenge for the students. There is a lot of confusion around Colleges, Courses and Consultancies which many times leads to wrong decisions. At Nascent Education Foundation we understand the problem and therefore each Admission season we carry out multiple Awareness Events all around Birgunj and other parts of Nepal. In these events through our professional experience as an consultancy we bring the Colleges and Students on the same platform where they can get to know each other better. Students can stroll around a number of Colleges and talk to their representatives and get a better understanding about the course of their choice. This eliminates the chances of all the Fraud consultancies who want to trick the students.

Every year Nascent Education Foundation the best consultancy of birgunj brings many colleges to these events. We have direct relations with these colleges. The colleges very graciously accept our offer and visit Nepal. The Event is named as the Indian Education Fair. It is organised every year by NEF in Birgunj and many other cities also.

The 4th Indian Education Fair-2019 is a platform for all the students aspiring for better tomorrow and cost-effective opportunities to all colleges/universities.The students get a first hand experience of the universities and the college gets to expand their international recruiting efforts by meeting and interacting with a large number of Nepalese students and educators.This educational exhibition ensures right platform for Universities/Colleges and Parents/Students. The students at the Fair can directly talk to the represntatives of the college and get information about all the formalities and requirements for getting admission in their institute. Nascent Education Foundation focuses to bring in the best options from India of all faculties in these fairs, so that all students from all streams can come down to the fair and get the colleges of their choice.

These fairs are not only events but they are also an opportunity fot he students to get exposure about the different opportunities that lie ahead. These aevents are free to join and every student and parent must visit such events. Nascent Education Foundation has been working hard to organize such events around the country, so that no place gets left behind. In the year 2020 also we plan on organizing many such events. For more information on such events you can visit us at Birgunj Office or connect through facebook.
