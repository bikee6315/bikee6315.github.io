Entrance Preparation

Nascent Education Foundation is an Educational Consultancy which believes in building a strong foundation for the students and therefor we provide multiple Entrance Preparations for our students so that they excel in their respective Educational Environments. Entrance exams have become an important part of all Educational institutes and are used for defining merits. We provide the best in-class Entrance preparation at our Locations, From SEE to Bridge Courses we provide support for all.


Study in India

India is one of the best Education hub and favourite for Nepalese students. Nascent Education Foundation or NEF provides best platform to get connected with the world class colleges and universities of India for higher education. Nascent Education Foundation as an Educational Consultant provides the best support to the students who want to study in Inida. Our Educational Consultnacy provides Documentation support, Travel assistance, Campus visits for parents and many other services which make us the Best Consultancy offering Study in India.

Study in Bangladesh

All Medical and Nursing students have renamed Bangladesh as their Educational Home for Medical Education. Medical Study in BAngladesh is Affordable and is top Quality. Nascent Education Foundation Educational Consultancy offers top Medical Colleges and Universities of Bangladesh for making career in MBBS or BDS. The Proximity of Nepal and Bangladesh in multiple aspects make it the Best destination for students. Our Educational Consultancy offers Bangladesh Education under the Study in Bangladesh banner.


Scholarships

Financial Restrictions are one of the major reasons why many students fail to procure higher education in the College of their dreams. As a second guardian, Nascent Education Foundation Educational Consultancy assists the students in procuring scholarships from College/Universities of India & Bangladesh. Our Educational consultancy helps the students through all the formalities and Documentation process. We regularly keep our students updated and make sure no Student is denied education due to Financial Causes.

Career Counselling

The Adolescent age is one of the most confusing yet crucial age of any student. Guidance can make or break any student's Career. Nascent Education Foundation as an Educational Consultancy believes in one to one counselling. We have an in-house team of trained counsellors and mentors who not only help the students in get over this confusion we guide them and help them on the path to their dream Career. Our Consultancy provides the options of only the Top College/Universities for the Career of your Dreams.


Study in China

China is one of the Global Superpowers and is one of the allies for Nepal. China's Education Department has been appreciated in Nepal by the students and many students aspire to got there for higher Education. Nascent Education Foundation is one of the few Educationsl Consultyancy that offers Study In China program. The Study in China program from our Educational Consultancy Nascent Education Foundation connects medical students from Nepal to pursue their higher studies in Top Universities/Colleges of China.
